In the sunny, warm and cozy village of bunnyville preparations are underway for the Great Yearly Feast of the Mighty Carrot. One of the key events of the feast is a Carrot Cake Baking competition, where all the best bunny bakers present their Ultimate Carrot Cake, and the winner is celebrated for the rest of the year for his carroty amazement. But, with only a couple of hours to the event, disaster strikes! Some of the competion cakes have been stolen!
